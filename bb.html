Why Donate Blood?

  • It saves lives
  • It helps patients with medical conditions
  • It's a simple and easy way to give back to the community
  • You can donate blood every 56 days

Who Can Donate?

  • Anyone who is 17 years or older and in good health
  • Individuals weighing at least 110 pounds
